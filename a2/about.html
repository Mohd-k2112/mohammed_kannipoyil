About 100SMILES Dental Care

100SMILES Dental Care is a family-friendly dental clinic serving our local community with care and trust. Located at 33 Fantail Street, Razole, Queensland, we provide affordable and professional dental care for patients of every age.

Our highly skilled team brings over 50 years of combined experience across general, preventive, cosmetic, children’s, and emergency dentistry. We focus on patient comfort by ensuring a calm and supportive environment, especially for young and anxious patients.

We proudly support the community through free dental check-up events, school awareness programs, and seasonal offers to make dental care more accessible.

Why Annual Dental Check-Ups Matter

Early Detection: Annual check-ups help dentists identify problems early before they become serious, leading to easier and more successful treatment.

Preventive Care: Routine cleanings, X-rays and check-ups protect you from major dental diseases and maintain long-term oral health.

Nutritional Guidance: Dentists provide tailored dietary advice that supports stronger teeth at every age.

Dental Health Evaluation: Your teeth and gums are examined to catch signs of disease early and maintain overall well-being.

Holistic Well-Being: Annual visits support mental comfort, hygiene routines and confidence through personalised dental guidance.

Behavioural Changes: Dentists can identify behaviour changes that may indicate deeper issues, ensuring early intervention and proper care.
