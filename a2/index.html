Friendly Dental Care for All Ages

Welcome to 100SMILES Dental Care, located at 33 Fantail Street, Razole, Queensland. Our experienced team brings over 50 years of combined experience in general, preventive, cosmetic, and emergency dentistry.

Our website is designed to make your visit easier. You can find our dental services, opening hours, staff details, event announcements, and you can book your appointments online.

We especially focus on children and families by providing a calm, supportive, and friendly environment that helps young patients feel safe and confident.

Child-Focused & Family Friendly

At 100SMILES Dental Care, we understand that a child's first dental visit can feel scary. That is why our team uses gentle communication, simple explanations, and a friendly approach to make dental visits comfortable for both children and parents.

  • Gentle, child-friendly treatment style
  • Easy-to-understand explanations that reduce dental fear
  • Preventive care that supports lifelong healthy habits
  • Clear, helpful guidance for parents
