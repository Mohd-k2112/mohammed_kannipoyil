Special Care For Children

Children are our priority. We provide child-friendly services that reduce fear, build confidence and create positive habits from an early age. Our pediatric dentist uses gentle techniques and clear explanations so both children and parents feel comfortable.

  • Gentle, child-centred communication
  • Short, comfortable procedures designed for kids
  • Parental guidance and toothbrushing coaching
  • Fun, calming environment for younger visitors

Annual Dental Check-Up Package

Our Annual Dental Check-Up package (currently available with a limited-time discount) is designed to give a thorough review of your oral health and keep small problems from growing into larger ones.

Physical Examination:

Complete examination of teeth, gums and oral tissues to check for issues or abnormalities.

Professional Cleanings:

Thorough tartar and plaque removal to protect your gums and reduce risk of disease.

X-Rays (Digital):

Low-radiation imaging for early detection of hidden problems.

Dental Check-Up:

Detailed review and personalised advice on oral health and prevention.

Nutritional Guidance:

Diet advice to support healthy teeth for children and adults.

Blood Tests or Laboratory Work (If Necessary):

Additional diagnostic tests when needed to ensure complete care.

Our New Dental Technologies

Digital X-Rays

High-resolution images with lower radiation for faster and more accurate diagnosis.

Intraoral Cameras

Detailed intraoral images that help you see exactly what the dentist sees.

Laser Dentistry

Minimally invasive procedures with reduced discomfort and faster healing.

CAD/CAM Technology

Design and manufacture custom crowns/veneers in-clinic for faster restorations.

3D Printing

Accurate models, surgical guides and appliances made quickly and precisely.

Remote consultations and follow-ups — convenient care from home.
