About Our Clinic

100SMILES Dental Care has been part of the Razole, Queensland community for over three decades. We are dedicated to offering gentle, professional, and affordable dental care for patients of all ages.

Whether it's a child's first dental visit or an adult's regular check-up, our team ensures a comfortable and welcoming environment every time. Our clinic is equipped with modern technology to provide the highest standard of dental treatments.

We aim to make every patient feel relaxed, confident, and well cared for during their visit. Our experienced staff and friendly atmosphere reflect our promise: “Healthy Smiles Start Here.”

Our Mission

To provide high-quality dental care with compassion, integrity, and respect — helping every patient and children achieve a healthy, confident smile that lasts a lifetime.
